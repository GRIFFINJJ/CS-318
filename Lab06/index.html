Discoveries of the James Webb Telescope

Our Galaxy and Beyond Our Solar System

The successful launch of the James Webb telescope has allowed the viewing of distances and times long ago. The telescope works primarily in infrared, scanning planets both in our solar system, and astral bodies far beyond. This website hopes to capture a few of the more important images that the James Webb Space Telescope has provided since its Launch on December 25, 2021.
